Our Philosophy

Our goal is to exceed our customer’s expectations by ensuring quality and excellence in every aspect of our business. We do this by providing global logistics services, multimode transportation and customs brokerage for our customers in a cost effective and timely fashion.

Our highly specialised staff work together to provide a service that is carefully organized, closely monitored, and aimed at giving a complete and first class client orientated service.

No matter how large or small the freight, whether it is a one-off or a regular consignment, we can manage the whole process for you while you concentrate on your core activities.

The Team

Our Logistics Team is trained and specialised in meeting your urgent or routine requirements. They will help you evaluate your shipping options, making recommendations to reduce time cycles, and will tailor make solutions to meet your precise transport and logistics needs. They will help you ship anything, and to any corner of the world with greater confidence. Every shipment is a chance to showcase our know-how.

We carry General Cargo, Live Animals, Perishable Cargo, Dangerous Goods, Valuables, Pharmaceuticals, High Value Electronics, and any type of time sensitive cargo…the list is as long as your imagination, you name it, we carry it!

We are confident that once you choose to work with us, you will be completely satisfied. Let SMS Logistics take care of your cargoes.
