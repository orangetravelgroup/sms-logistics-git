Project Logistics

SMS Logistics is also dedicated to providing logistic solutions for major manufacturers, construction or engineering projects. Specialized staff at SMS Logistics will offer a solution for any type of projects worldwide expediting, heavy-lift transport, charters and deliveries

Project Logistics involves detailed door-to-door planning and customized transport support. The service starts with assessment and consultancy on every aspect of a cargo movement and finishes with delivery and, if necessary, on-site positioning. SMS Logistics has the necessary resources to translate a "can do" approach into effective action.
