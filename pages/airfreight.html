Air Freight

SMS Logistics provides Airfreight services worldwide, through its agents and partners.

Having established itself as one of the leading players amongst the local freight forwarders, SMS Logistics offer:

  1. A comprehensive range of services for inbound and outbound freight transport by air

  2. Worldwide operations through its agents and partners

  3. Priority handling and competitive rates thanks to long-standing relationships with all the major air carriers

  4. Sophisticated tracking and tracing.

Through its agents and partners, SMS Logistics has a network offering comprehensive and cost-effective services to all corners of the globe and a full range of value added services. All representing agents and partners, everywhere in the world, have their own customs brokerage licences for the region in which they operate. As a standard procedure we always guarantee the following services with every shipment, no matter the size.

  1. Immediate pick-up and booking of freight at origin

  2. Checking of all shipping and customs documentation

  3. Status updates from the time of collection, during transit, and up to point of delivery

  4. Pre-alert notification of all flight details with copies of the relevant documents via e-mail/fax upon uplift

  5. Customs declaration and clearance at both origin and destination

  6. Delivery up to the door-step

  7. Proof of delivery upon shipment arrival and delivery


The Charter Option

At times, commercial uplift may be very limited, especially when Malta is only served with few freighter operations. This is why chartering additional air freight capacity is part of our everyday business. This may happen, for example, when customers launch new products, during peak seasons, or when oversized cargo is being handled. To solve the problem, SMS can provide tailor-made solutions where you can choose to charter an entire aircraft.

The Customer Benefits

  3. High flexibility

  4. Defined transit time statement
