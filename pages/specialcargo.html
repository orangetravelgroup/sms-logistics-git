Special Cargo

At SMS we have extensive airfreight experience with special cargo. Having only the best qualified staff in the industry all shipments are handled according to the IATA regulations to ensure your cargo reaches the destination safely and in a timely manner.


Live Animals

We are also experienced in handling live animals by air. Such handling is taken care of by our IATA qualified staff, having years of experience in this filed.

The carriage of any live animals must comply with the respective IATA Live Animals Regulations, as well as the country of destination. These regulations specify the design elements of containers for the animals, documentation, labeling, and handling recommendation.


Perishable Cargo

These are goods that will deteriorate over a given period of time, if exposed to a certain temperature. We handle the broadest range of perishables at any given time of day to ensure your shipment is delivered safely.


Dangerous Goods

The preparation of such shipments for transport is of utmost importance be it by air road or sea. Our highly trained and licensed staff ensures that the acceptance, loading and handling of dangerous goods complies with international transport and established regulations. These regulations specify how the articles must by packed, marked, labeled, and documented.
