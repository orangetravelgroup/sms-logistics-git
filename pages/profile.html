Profile

SMS Group is a well-established and diversified group of companies, consisting of Maritime, Travel, Tourism, Insurance, Logistics, Supplies, Corporate, Technology, and Hospitality divisions. SMS Group’s trading origins may be traced back as far as 1860, but it was in 1950 that the current family-owned business S. Mifsud & Sons Ltd. which was established by Chev. Comm. Silvio C. Mifsud as Malta’s foremost Shipping Agency.

The Group’s trading tradition spans over two centuries and the SMS name is synonymous with pioneering spirit and innovation throughout the Maltese Islands. Leadership, expansion and achievement are all hallmarks of the SMS reputation.

SMS Group currently operates under the Chairmanship of Neville Mifsud who is assisted by the directors, Dr. Simon Mifsud, Samuel Mifsud, Michael Warrington, Joe Cappello and Dr. Lorraine Conti. Together, they are assisted by a complement of more than 350 staff and generate an annual turnover of US$30m. The Group is a healthy and diversified multi-national organization with a highly competitive market share spread out across its diverse range of activities.
