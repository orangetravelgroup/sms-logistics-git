Industries

At SMS we have extensive airfreight experience with special cargo. Having only the best qualified staff in the industry all shipments are handled according to the IATA regulations to ensure your cargo reaches the destination safely and in a timely manner.


Automotive

The logistics challenge for the automotive manufacturing industry is to reduce the enormous volume of parts inventory to minimum levels while ensuring that no shortage occurs for any single part. This is a very difficult challenge considering the enormous number of parts required, as well as the fact that many of these parts are sourced from various suppliers spread out globally.

At SMS Logistics we are capable of providing a range of solutions to meet these challenges while considering each customer's specific business requirements.


Retail

SMS Logistics is in a position to offer services for many types of retailers, including designer and high street fashion, electronics, as well as supermarkets. SMS Logistics has the depth of knowledge and breadth of services to ensure that consumer demand is met, wherever and whenever it exists.

With national network coverage SMS Logistics is capable to manage home delivery solutions which are focused on helping retailers service phone and internet-based retailing. Our aim is to fulfil our customers promise to their customers by providing a high quality and cost efficient home delivery service.


Consumer Electronics

SMS Logistics offers a total package of supply chain services developed in line with the specific needs of consumer electronics companies. Services range from, importation, storage and Customs Clearance, through to delivery to the end user.


Healthcare

SMS Logistics has in-depth experience throughout the healthcare supply chain from the delivery of raw materials into the manufacturing process, to the delivery of finished drugs, be it by air, road, or sea.

Through our skilled staff and extensive know-how SMS Logistics is capable to support the entire healthcare supply chain.
