Road Freight

SMS Logistics offers both import and export road transportation covering the whole of Europe.
Our substantial volumes and buying power combined with excellent relations with the best local trailer operators, enables us to offer our customers:

  1. Part and Full Load Services (Dry and Temperature controlled)

  2. Highly competitive rates

  3. Guaranteed weekly departures

  4. Reliable and reduced transit times

Multiple pick ups can be organized together with the facility of storage to enable consolidation of shipments.

Temperature controlled boxes may also be provided for the carriage of any temperature sensitive cargo be it part or full load.

As a standard procedure we always guarantee the following services with every shipment, no matter the size.

  1. Immediate pick-up and booking of freight at origin

  2. Checking of all shipping and customs documentation

  3. Status updates from the time of collection, during transit, and up to point of delivery

  4. Pre-alert notification of all shipping details with copies of the relevant documents via e-mail/fax upon uplift

  5. Customs declaration and clearance at both origin and destination

  6. Delivery up to the door-step

  7. Proof of delivery upon shipment arrival and delivery
